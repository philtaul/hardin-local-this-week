About this data

The Hardin Local Weekly Housing Market Update is produced each week by Rachel Brantingham of The Brantingham Group (KW). Figures reflect activity in Elizabethtown and Hardin County, Kentucky.

Methodology notes

  • Days on market is a 30-day rolling average — how long homes are taking to go under contract.
  • Months of inventory = active homes ÷ homes sold in the last 30 days. Under 5 = seller-leaning, over 6 = buyer-leaning, in between = balanced.
  • Weekly counts (new, under contract, closed) reflect the rolling seven days before each Tuesday livestream.
  • Gaps in the trend lines (E04, E08, E09, E15) are weeks where no housing segment aired.

Data disclaimer

These figures are compiled for public information. Countywide data — individual neighborhoods and price ranges may behave differently. For personal real-estate decisions, consult a licensed agent.
