🏇 Derby Week — Friday + Saturday

Kentucky Oaks Friday · Kentucky Derby Saturday

The 152nd Kentucky Derby runs Saturday, May 2 at Churchill Downs — post time ~6:57 PM ET. Peacock coverage starts at 12 PM ET, NBC + Peacock from 2:30 PM through the race.

🆕 First time ever — Oaks in primetime

Friday, May 1 — the Kentucky Oaks moves to a primetime broadcast for the first time in its 152-year history. NBC + Peacock primetime block 8:00 – 9:00 PM ET, post time ~8:40 PM ET.
🎩 Hat parties + viewing parties are scattered all across Hardin County — see Saturday's events below for our local Derby Day lineup, including our top pick: the Hat Contest at Artie Ray's Sundown Lounge in Vine Grove.
