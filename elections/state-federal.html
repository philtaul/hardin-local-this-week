U.S. Senate & State Offices

Primary: May 19, 2026 General: November 3, 2026

State and federal races aren't tracked on the Hardin County Clerk's local candidate filing list — they're administered by the Kentucky Secretary of State. Hardin Local invited every candidate we could identify in races that include Hardin County voters. The list below is who responded.

About this page. Because state and federal races aren't on the Hardin County Clerk filing list we use as source of truth for the rest of these pages, we may have missed candidates who filed with the state but did not appear on local outreach lists. For the official, complete ballot, please consult the Kentucky State Board of Elections. Order below is by office, then by ballot order where known.
