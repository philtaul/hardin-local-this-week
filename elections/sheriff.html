What happens after May 19

The winner of the Republican primary faces John Ward (D) in the November 3, 2026 general election. Hardin Local will revisit this race ahead of November — what that looks like depends on the field. Possibilities: a paid follow-up interview for any candidate who wants more airtime, a re-air of the primary interview, or — if both nominees are interested — a head-to-head debate. Either way, voters will hear from both sides.
