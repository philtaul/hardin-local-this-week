Radcliff City Council

Nonpartisan Primary · 14-way Primary: May 19, 2026 General: November 3, 2026

Fourteen candidates filed for six at-large seats. Kentucky law triggers a nonpartisan primary when filings exceed twice the number of open seats — so the May 19 primary will narrow the field. The top twelve finishers advance to the November 3, 2026 general election, where each Radcliff voter selects up to six.

About this page. Hardin Local invited every filed candidate in this race to a free 20-minute on-camera interview. Order below is by ballot position. Edited videos are added as they are produced; until then, you'll find a link to the live broadcast where applicable. Candidates who chose not to participate are still listed so you can see the full ballot.

How the May 19 ballot works

Each Radcliff voter selects up to six candidates on the May 19 primary ballot. The top twelve vote-getters advance to the November 3, 2026 general election, where the field narrows again to fill six open Council seats. Hardin Local will revisit this race ahead of November.
