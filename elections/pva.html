Property Valuation Administrator

Republican Primary · 3-way Democratic · Uncontested Primary: May 19, 2026 General: November 3, 2026

The PVA sets property assessments that drive tax bills across the county. Three Republicans are competing for the nomination on May 19. The Democratic nominee is unopposed in his primary.

About this page. Hardin Local invited every filed candidate in this race to a free 20-minute on-camera interview. Order below is by ballot position. Edited videos are added as they are produced; until then, you'll find a link to the live broadcast where applicable. Candidates who chose not to participate are still listed so you can see the full ballot.
