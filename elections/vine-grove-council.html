Vine Grove City Council

Nonpartisan No Primary Required General: November 3, 2026

Nine candidates filed for six at-large seats. Kentucky law triggers a nonpartisan primary only when filings exceed twice the number of open seats (in this case, more than 12). With nine filings, no primary is required — all nine candidates appear directly on the November 3, 2026 general-election ballot, where each voter selects up to six.

About this page. Because no primary is being held for this race, Hardin Local did not run pre-primary interviews. We plan to invite every filed candidate to a general-election interview later this fall. Order below is alphabetical by last name.

How the November ballot works

All nine candidates appear on the November 3, 2026 general-election ballot. Each Vine Grove voter may select up to six. The six highest vote-getters win the open seats. Hardin Local will invite every filed candidate to a fall interview ahead of the general election.
