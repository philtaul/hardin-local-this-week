Magistrate / Justice of the Peace

8 districts 5 contested R primaries Primary: May 19, 2026 General: November 3, 2026

Hardin County is divided into eight Magistrate (Justice of the Peace) districts. Each district elects its own representative to Fiscal Court — the body that controls the county budget, road funds, and many local ordinances. Use the menu below to jump to your district.

What happens after May 19

The winner of each contested primary faces the other party's nominee in the November 3, 2026 general election. Districts with no contested primary on either side go straight to the general. Hardin Local will revisit Magistrate races ahead of November — possibilities include paid follow-up interviews, re-airs of the primary interviews, or head-to-head debates if both nominees are interested.
